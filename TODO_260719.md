### Anonymisation design notes

- **What is sensitive:** 60 samples carry DMP-style (`C-0KNW20_CTCL76_3_N`) or
  accession-style (`S16-8625N`, `22-4426_T`, `23-83695_T`) identifiers, in
  `Proj_15966` (28), `Proj_17297_B` (24) and `ReMap_260130` (8). Decide
  separately whether project labels need masking too — `UMich` names an
  institution and `ReMap_260130` embeds a date.
- **IDs must be stable across re-imports** so adding a project does not renumber
  the existing background. A salted hash of `project::sample` truncated to a
  short token does this; a sequential counter does not.
- **Keep the crosswalk out of the repo** (or gitignored) — it is the thing that
  re-identifies.
- **Apply at write time**, `importQCBackground.R:163`, so all name-bearing
  outputs are covered: `backgroundSamples.tsv` and `backgroundFlagged.tsv` both
  carry raw names today. The stats files are aggregate and already safe.
- Preserve `sampleType` and the `project`+`sample` uniqueness; the reference
  ranges do not depend on names at all, so nothing else breaks.
